This is a collection of hopefully useful introductions, and complete guides to doing things. My goal is to avoid the Curse of Knowledge. We don't need yet another seasoned Haskell programmer describing monads.

This series will be written from the perspective of a girl with one or more "learning disabilities". This hasn't stopped me, despite the name, from learning. I just find most presentation lacking.

I like to understand things in depth, because I have trouble mentalizing things without knowing most of the abstractions underlying it. So these entries will answer the questions I wanted answers to and found hard to find, and hopefully answer them in a way that I would have found indespensible when I was exploring something new.
